## Overview

A transparent platform where indie hackers share their real revenue numbers. Learn from actual data, see what works, and understand the real economics of indie products.

## Features

- **Real Revenue Data** - Actual MRR and revenue numbers
- **Verified Entries** - Community-verified revenue claims
- **Growth Tracking** - See month-over-month growth rates
- **Tech Stack Tags** - See what technologies successful products use
- **Category Filtering** - Filter by product category
- **Sorting Options** - Sort by revenue, growth, or age

![Revenue Entry](./../../screenshots/revenue-hub/revenue-entry.png)

![Filtering Interface](./../../screenshots/revenue-hub/filtering.png)

## How It Works

1. **Browse** - Explore real revenue data from indie products
2. **Filter** - Find products by category, tech stack, or growth rate
3. **Learn** - Understand what makes products successful
4. **Share** - Add your own revenue data to help others

## Stats Dashboard

- **Total MRR Tracked** - Combined MRR of all products
- **Average Growth** - Mean growth rate across all products
- **Total Products** - Number of products sharing data
- **Monthly Views** - Community engagement metrics

![Stats Dashboard](./../../screenshots/revenue-hub/stats-dashboard.png)

## Data Points

Each entry includes:
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Growth percentage (month-over-month)
- Product category
- Tech stack used
- Product age
- Founder information
- Verification status

## Filtering Options

- **All** - See all revenue entries
- **Verified** - Only verified revenue claims
- **Trending** - Products with 20%+ growth

## Sorting Options

- By Revenue (highest first)
- By Growth (fastest growing first)
- By Age (newest first)
